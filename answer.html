Tips for Answering Questions

  1. Utilise your reading time to plan out your answers.
  2. Identify the task word in the question (meanings all vary depending on the subject), these include:

    • Explain
    • Define
    • Outline
    • Describe
    • Distinguish
    • Evaluate
    • Identify
    • ETCETERA
  3. Identify the concept/s.
  4. Identify the allocated marks.
  5. Determine what the question is asking.
