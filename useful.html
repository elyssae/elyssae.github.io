Useful Websites and Apps

These useful websites will help you improve your study experience. Brainscape provides a digital platform for creating and studying flashcards, as well as the ability to organise and grade your comprehension of each card. Notion is an excellent tool for remaining organised, assisting you in managing timetables, to-do lists, and vital information. Grammarly ensures perfect grammar and clarity in your writing, while Quilbot offers tools for paraphrasing, summarising, and plagiarism detection. Using these strong online resources, you can streamline your studies and increase your efficiency.

Brainscape

If you like studying through flashcards you need to try Brainscape, it is a digital website and app that allows you to create, organise, and study flashcards. Brainscape allows teachers and students to study flashcards anywhere through their mobile app as well as study others' flashcards. When you study the flashcards you can further categorise them from 1 - 5, one meaning you don't understand it at all, three somewhat, and five meaning perfectly. At the end of studying a set, you will receive a percentage of how much you understand the set this helps to find the gaps in your knowledge. Alternatively, you could also purchase a pro account which provides you with more features such as images on your flashcards. Overall, Brainsacpe is one of the best websites/apps to use when creating flashcards.

Notion

Notion is an amazing app for organisation. You can create your own personal page with timetables, to-do lists, calendars, etc. You can also download templates made by others and modify them to your liking. This app helps to hold you accountable for not just study-related things but normal day-to-day activities. For instance, you might have wanted to start a habit of going to the gym this app can hold you accountable to tick it off your list. It also helps organise information like your teacher's contact details, assignments, and due dates. Overall, Notion motivates individuals to achieve their goals through organisation.

Grammarly

Grammarly is a very popular website/app that ensures the grammar of your work. This is very helpful for English-based classes (which are the majority of them) and even non-English-based classes. This is because it also ensures the grammar of your emails, terminology, and fluency which helps the teacher understand the contents. Subsequently, Grammarly ensures the grammar of students' and teachers' work which ensures the receiver can understand the information.

Quilbot

Quilbot is a website that allows students and teachers to paraphrase, summarise, and plagiarism check. Paraphrasing helps individuals to expand their vocabulary and ensure their work is connected to their form/tone. Summarising is extremely useful for flashcards because it helps condense your notes into key knowledge.
